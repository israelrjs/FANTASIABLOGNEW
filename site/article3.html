Top Affordable KC Restaurants That You Must Try

Created by Fantasia Wesley Saturday, December 7, 2019 6:59PM

You may know Kansas City for their Visiting Kansas City could be one of the greatest experiences ever, especially if you’re a food ranger. Trying something new could reward you with potential growth, a broader mindset, and a full, satisfied stomach. The restaurants that will be listed is less expensive, get more for your buck, delicious but affordable restaurants. There will be additional articles on the more high-end restaurants as well, wings, BBQ, and more so stay connected and subscribe to read future articles!


Woodyard Bar-B-Que

Address: 3001 Merriam Ln, Kansas City, KS 66106 | P: (913) 362-8000


Those of you who know, know. Woodyard Bar-B-Que offers They offer the authentic, classic taste in Kansas City Bar- B-Que you’re looking for. Anything between burnt ends, to the delectable, juicy and thick wings that are flavored and seasoned to perfection, and their creamy corn that finishes any meal. It all started in 2005, Frank Schloegel decided to turn his wood supply store since 1913 into a functional restaurant. Please make sure to get their creamy corn, it’ll put the topping on any meal you get.



Red Door

leawoodcamelot court | 11851 roe ave. | leawood, ks 66211 | 913-227-4959


Red Door is a great place to go with your friends and family to enjoy the great environment that the restaurant carries. The food is absolutely amazing, with a wide range of different options to choose from. When I went, the chicken alfredo (I’m not 100%) was delicious. The lively aura makes you feel important, with great vibes. I don’t remember much about the music, but the servers were on point with the service and is highly recommended.



Café Gratitude

(816) 474-5683 333 Southwest Blvd, Kansas City, MO 64108


If you’re vegan, this is your spot! If you’re hungry, this is your spot! If you’re curious, this is your spot! I was introduced to this restaurant by a friend, and I was extremely unaware of how good vegan food actually was. The nachos are absolutely delicious, and the “cheese” actually tastes like dairy product. Weird right? It’s only nuts! If you’re downtown, at the plaza or Westport you should definitely stop by and try it out! You definitely won’t regret it!



Buca di Beppo Italian Restaurant

310 W 47th St, Kansas City, MO 64112 (816) 931-6548


Now, Buca di Beppo may not be everyone’s favorite, but it sure is mine! If you are looking for more affordable (but not Olive Garden) Italian food, then this is your option! You can literally share every single plate that comes out with at least one additional person. The platters are humongous so you will not leave with an empty stomach but save room for deserts because they have plenty!



RA Sushi

11638 Ash St, Leawood, KS 66211 (913) 850-6260


This is one of the best sushi joints you can go and enjoy your favorite California rolls. This restaurant not only has the great customer service, but the music is absolutely wonderful. If you aren’t fond to eating with loud music, then I wouldn’t suggest this restaurant for you. The fresh sushi bar always stays busy with customers because the food proves the quality is worthy. Give this sushi bar a go, it will be a bang for your buck!



Peach Tree

6800 Eastwood Trafficway, Kansas City, MO 64129 (816) 923-0099


Now you may be thinking what this is. This here my friend is one of the greatest soul food buffets in the history of soul food buffets. Not only is it affordable, but it is absolutely delicious. From the yams, to the fried chicken, everything is cooked to perfection. The seasoning in the food taste like it was made by my grandmother herself. The soulful touch to the restaurant is great for any family event, and if you’re lucky, it may be a date.



Hawaiian Bros

12120 College Blvd, Overland Park, KS 66210
1112 E North Ave, Belton, MO 64012


Hawaiian food in Kansas City? Trust it. It is wonderful. The savory and sweet taste is completely undeniably good. The quality, taste, and appealing looks will have you coming back for seconds. Do not hesitate to go, it may be new for you, but it is worth the journey.



Blue Sushi Sake

4705 Rainbow Blvd, Westwood, KS 66205 (913) 236-6100


Yes, sushi is my favorite food and it should be yours too! Have you experienced a tropical, mellow, but lively sushi bar in Kansas City? They have all of the nostalgic feels you are looking for in a California feel. One of the best features of this particular Sushi bar is their happy hour. Their happy hour isn’t stingy! Even on weekends, there is always a deal. If you’re planning on getting a drink with your meal, this is definitely the place to go.



Lidia’s

101 W 22nd St, Kansas City, MO 64108 (816) 221-3722


Lidia’s has a great classical feel. The food is elegant, and the aesthetic is rather pleasing. I enjoyed this restaurant. When I went, they had an unlimited serving or some pastas and other dishes. They had some of the best fried calamari that I have ever tasted. A wonderful experience that everyone should try out! Get a reservation with them for a special occasion, its fully worth it!




These are some of the best restaurants that I have been to with affordable prices and a bang for your buck! There are plenty of other restaurants in Kansas City that you may know about, that was or was not on this fairly short list, but I will include a vary of different restaurants in a different article for best Bar- B-Que restaurants, best Wing joints, and more! So, make sure you stay tuned, and give a comment of your best affordable restaurant in town!
