The Power of Manifestation and How to Conquer It

Article by Fantasia Wesley - Sunday, December 15th, 2019 6:30PM CST

The Power of Manifestation… What do you think of when you hear that? Well, the definition of Manifestation “an event, action, or object that clearly shows or embodies something, especially a theory or an abstract idea.” – Oxford Dictionary - https://www.lexico.com/en/definition/manifestation




In other words, you have an idea, you think the idea through, and you execute the idea to the best extent. I have a personal experience that I had witnessed myself of someone using the power of manifestation to their best potential and ended up exactly where they wanted to be. A man by the name of JP Rand- https://www.instagram.com/jp_rand/ became the legendary; Lil Kim, one of the best female rapper’s hairstylist. I wanted to hear more about his story, so when I was in an internship with the radio station Hot 103.3 Jamz, I had the opportunity to bring him on for the podcast “People Places Things Podcast” with host Shay Moore. When he came, he brought up that he manifested the moments that he now has as a celebrity hairstylist. JP brought up multiple points on how he used to dream of doing Lil Kim’s hair. JP than began to work as hard as he could to get that dream to become a reality. He said “Growing up, I did not know much about her. My age group was Nicki Minaj, you know so when the comparison thing came about, and then I had like older siblings. So, they told me… I started to engage with her, she was hard.” He also said that he would start imagining himself doing Lil Kim’s hair. He mentioned that he would say “I am going to do Lil Kim’s hair” and before he knew it, he was on a plane headed to her.


It’s all within your mental capacity and capabilities. It is also referred to as the Self-Fulfilling Prophecy. You are able to predict your outcomes, simply because you believe it. You have to start believing in yourself first. When you believe in yourself, you won’t need additional acceptance or approval from anyone else. You have to believe that you will be in that position and speak it into existence. I say all the time that I will be the next Oprah Winfrey but be myself while doing it. Obviously, I can’t become Oprah, but looking to her as inspiration and studying the different methods of success, I do believe that I can do it. I will do it. The next step is to treat yourself as if you are in that position already. Don’t wait to become happy once you are in that position, then that would mean you wouldn’t be happy at all to try. You need to start now. Appreciate the position you are in. Own it. But work harder also. You need to understand that working hard is the only way you can and will succeed, meaning multiple sacrifices. I don’t mean sell your soul and sacrifice your family members. I’m talking about the sacrifice that prevents you from doing what you want and doing what you need. You need to understand that it will contain hardship along the way, but it’ll all be worth it. Sometimes may be fun, while others can be difficult to pass. But it will all pass and all the hard work you put into it will work in your favor. Believe in yourself, and do not think about what others may think.
